    Invited to Return to Piper Jaffray Conference

    We’re honored to announce that Surf Canyon has been invited to participate in the Web Discovery & Search Panel at Piper Jaffray’s Fifth Annual Global Internet Summit to be held at the Rosewood Sand Hill in Menlo Park on November 10th and 11th.

    This is the second year in a row that Surf Canyon has participated on a panel at the GIS. Last year’s conference was a great success and this year Mark Cramer will be sharing the panel with Paul Forster from Indeed, Venky Harinarayan from Kosmix, Ted West from LookSmart, Tobias Peggs from OneRiot and Nancy Harvey from Wolfram Research. Once again, the panel discussion will be moderated by Gene Munster, Piper Jaffray Senior Research Analyst.
