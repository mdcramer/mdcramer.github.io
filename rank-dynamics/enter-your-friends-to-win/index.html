    Update (8/26/08) - This will be the last week for this promotion. The final winner will be announced tomorrow, August 27th, 2008. Thanks to everyone who participated!

    This week Surf Canyon is initiating a contest to promote the new “Email This” feature. A $25 gift certificate to Amazon.com will be distributed to a lucky winner, selected every Wednesday via a weekly drawing, for as long as the contest runs. To enter a friend to win, simply use the “Email This” feature to send him or her a search result, with contest@surfcanyon.com on copy.
